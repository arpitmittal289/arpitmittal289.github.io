About

Born and raised in India, Arpit earned his Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Engineering in 2016 from the Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Delhi (GGSIPU).
Throughout his undergraduate studies, he has been part of various CS Related Projects, in the domain of ML, AI, Android, Java etc.

Post completion of his Undergrad Degree, Arpit joined Nagarro, as a Software Developer, where he worked as a Full Stack Developer, with major tech Stack as JAVA J2EE as Backend, JSP,JS(JQuery),CSS for frontend and MYSQL for Database.

He successfully progressed Step by step from a Junior role to Technical Lead role for his projects with his Leadership and Team Management qualities, having a complete inderstanding of the projects. He was involved in Various Multi-Market E-Commerce website projects , and was responsible for modelling, implementing, deploying and maintainance of various features across the website after carefull understanding of the Requirements through client interaction.

In 2021, Arpit began pursuing his Master’s degree in Computer Science at the University of Southern California (USC), Los Angeles, where he focused his coursework in the domain of Artificial Intelligence.
At USC, he is also affiliated with the Information Sciences Insitute as a Machine Learning Engineer, working on Image synthesis along with Text modalities.
